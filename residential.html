Multi-Stage Separation Path

Our premium filters utilize a sequential mechanical and chemical separation path to remove contaminants while preserving essential trace minerals.

  1. Sediment Interception: High-density poly-spun fibers filter out rust, sand, dirt, and suspended particles.
  2. Carbon Block Adsorption: Activated coconut shell carbon adsorbs chlorine, trihalomethanes (THMs), and VOCs.
  3. Molecular RO Filtration: Pre-washed polyamide membranes remove heavy metals (Lead, Arsenic, Chromium) and micro-solutes.
  4. UV-C Sterilization: Instant UV-C light eliminates 99.99% of bacteria and viruses in real-time.
  5. Mineral Remineralization: Calcite and corosex media add calcium and magnesium trace ions for a fresh, crisp taste.
