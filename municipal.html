Intake to Distribution Flow

Protecting public health requires an uninterrupted, multi-barrier purification framework that monitors quality indicators continuously.

  • 01
    Coagulation & Flocculation: Flash mixers inject alum polymers to force tiny particles to cluster into larger masses (floc).
  • 02
    Sedimentation Clarification: Floc settled at gravity basements leaves clean, pre-clarified water at the surface weir.
  • 03
    Ultrafiltration Barriers: Polymeric capillary membranes filter out viruses, bacteria, and residual particles up to 0.02 microns.
  • 04
    Residual Disinfection: Controlled chlorine dosing secures microbiological protection throughout the pipeline network.
