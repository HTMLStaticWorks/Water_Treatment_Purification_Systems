Industrial Division

Industrial Water Treatment

High-throughput purification systems designed to achieve rigorous processing specifications and secure regulatory permits. Perfect for heavy manufacturing, mining, and food production plants.

Core Sub-systems:

  • RO Systems: Industrial reverse osmosis modules with up to 98% salt rejection.
  • Wastewater Treatment: Biological reactors and active clarification networks.
  • Effluent Treatment: Chemical oxidation systems for organic pollutant reduction.
  • Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD): Thermal evaporators to reuse 100% of wastewater.
