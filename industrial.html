Separation & Purification Process

Our industrial treatment loops rely on pressurized cross-flow separation. By driving water against dense membranes, solutes stay behind while purified permeate moves forward.

  1. Raw Pre-treatment: Multimedia quartz and activated carbon filters reduce suspended solids and scale potentials.
  2. Chemical Dosing: Anti-scalant dosing prevents calcium scale formation on membranes.
  3. High-Pressure separation: Advanced multistage pumps push the feed water past the semi-permeable boundary.
  4. Brine Recirculation: A portion of the concentrate is recirculated to maximize recovery, while the rest goes to ZLD thermal blocks.

Engineering Quality Safeguards

  • Fouling Prevention: Integrated Clean-in-Place (CIP) systems run automatically to flush scales.
  • Energy Recovery: Isobaric chambers capture feed-brine energy, cutting system power use by 30%.
  • Corrosion Control: Wet parts and piping are constructed of Stainless Steel 316L or Duplex Steel.
