Our Heritage

Engineering Pure Resource Cycles

Established in 2001, AQUATECH designs high-performance water filtration grids and complex purification membranes to satisfy global processing requirements.

Our organization started with a single engineering team dedicated to solving municipal hard water problems. Today, we stand as an international design brand with system installations crossing 35 countries, delivering clean processing water for pharmaceuticals, microelectronics, municipal networks, and households.

We strictly construct water systems matching ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 specifications, integrating dynamic IoT flow analytics into every custom layout to save energy and secure zero waste.

The Mission

Driving Sustainable Water Security

We aim to counter clean-water scarcity trends by designing mechanical RO systems with high water recovery rates, allowing heavy industrial processing loops to consume less raw groundwater resources.

Quality Standards

Precision Engineering Excellence

Our systems are fabricated using marine-grade stainless steels, high-density polyethylene pipelines, and IoT remote-telemetry controllers. Every weld, connection, and membrane block is pressure audited to guarantee zero failures.

Global Commitment

Zero Liquid Discharge Focus

Through Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) technologies, we enable processing facilities to reclaim up to 98% of waste brine. This minimizes municipal pollution hazards and protects local waterways from chemical run-offs.
